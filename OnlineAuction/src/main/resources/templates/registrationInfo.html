Course Registration at SRU

Registration for courses at Slippery Rock University is completed through the MySRU portal.

Registration Information:

  • Summer 2024 and Fall 2024 registration begins April 8, 2024.
  • The class schedule for Summer 2024 and Fall 2024 will be available March 22, 2024.
  • Winter 2024 and Spring 2025 registration begins November 11, 2024.
  • The class schedule for Winter 2024 and Spring 2025 will be available October 25, 2024.
  • For important dates and deadlines, please refer to the Academic Calendars.
  • For information on what Summer Session has to offer, visit the Summer Session webpage.
  • University Catalogs are available from the navigation menu on the left of this page or from the Academics tab under Related Links.
  • Class and Final Exam Schedules can be found below and in MySRU on the Academics Tab.
  • For information on undergraduate degree requirements visit the Academic Policies section within the catalog.
